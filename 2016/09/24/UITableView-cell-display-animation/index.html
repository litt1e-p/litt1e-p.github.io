UITableView cell display animation

2016-09-24

when will fire the animate effect?

there are 2 moments for it as below options:

  1. in - (void)tableView:(UITableView *)tableView willDisplayCell:(UITableViewCell *)cell forRowAtIndexPath:(NSIndexPath *)indexPath

  2. in -(void)viewDidAppear

I prefer to choose the second one cause it will fire the animation on everytime that view come appear
